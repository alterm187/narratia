## üé® Feature Highlights

### The "Merge Effect"
The portrait doesn't sit "on top of" the page - it's integrated into the background through:
- Multi-layer glow (large blur creates ambient light)
- Gradient rings (golden outer, dark inner)
- Reduced background opacity (5% vs 30%)
- Smooth color transitions
- Depth layering

### Always Visible
- **Old**: Hidden on mobile (`hidden lg:block`)
- **New**: Visible on all devices with smart ordering

### Professional Polish
- Subtle animations (not overwhelming)
- Hover interactions
- Premium feel
- Brand-consistent colors
